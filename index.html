Hello, this is Gravit:
Your new best Design Tool

Let me introduce gravit: an easy to handle but powerful app for web and print designs. Built to provide a fluid, fast and flexible way to realise all your creative ideas. Simply a perfect tool for the professional designers of today.

Detail in design

Gravit was build from the ground up to combine powerful functionality together with the approximate usability possible. Paste-inside, powerful typography tools and a full context-based user interface let you focus on your design and not on unnecessary details.

Simply beautiful

Utilize Gravit’s versatile styling system to create simply beautiful and awesome designs. Save work by sharing styles, quickly mix astonishing new colors and apply powerful multiple effects to polish your designs even more.

Tools to serve

Once having used Gravit’s powerful shape and path tools you will never want to look back again. Quickly create adjustable rectangles, polygons & ellipses. Go further by smoothing path edges automatically and apply vector effects to enhance your designs even more.
Gravit’s awesome path, shape and text tools allows you to quickly create designs limited only by your imagination. Save precious time with useful styling attributes and powerful raster and vector effects.
Gravit’s unique user interface and smart functionality offers great flexibility while being easy and intuitive. Get the right tool only in the context it is used in and see things working just the way you expect without getting in the way.
Add great animations, interactivity and responsiveness to your designs and preview them on the web or mobile. Go even further by sharing assets with your team and collaborate with your customers.
