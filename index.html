Unleash your creativity

Introducing Gravit, the new and fresh thinking free design tool to express yourself in a new way.

In Beta. Released August 2014.
Get informed when it's out!

Unlock your design potential

Meet Gravit – the cutting-edge design app that will take your creativity to new heights. Gravit offers the creative possibilities of a full-scale design suite - but in a snug app-sized package. Powerful yet easy-to-handle, Gravit has been custom designed from the ground up with an emphasis on versatility, fluidity and elegance – complex design tasks are made simple through its robust suite of tools and highly responsive smart work environment. Express yourself in a new way with Gravit – the new must-have tool for today’s pioneering design professionals!

Simplicity

Gravit offers an intuitive context-sensitive user interface which responds dynamically to your work process, without sacrificing one iota of functionality or capability. The right tools present themselves as required – the result? An uncluttered, smart design environment that is a real pleasure to use.

Elegance

Gravit’s advanced style-sharing features make it possible to share design assets between multiple documents, unifying your designs and saving you time. And creating those assets has never been easier - from common tasks like color palette selection to more advanced techniques like applying multiple-layered visual effects, our custom-designed styling system allows you to create complex and elegant designs with ease. A potent combination of simplicity and power – at every step.

Power

Gravit’s selection of powerful shape and path tools offer a range of possibilities that is second to none, enabling you to achieve complex effects from the simplest of components. Create and adjust rectangles, polygons and ellipses with ease, then apply automatic path smoothing, advanced vector effects – watch as your designs grow in complexity and daring, while remaining fully adjustable and responsive.
Gravit is a tailor-made blend of powerful functionality and ease of use, custom-built to help you maximise your creative potential. Features like robust typography tools, paste-inside support, and a versatile context-sensitive user interface have all been laid out with efficiency in mind. A streamlined work space means a fluid workflow and a greater focus on what really matters – your creativity.
Your talents take flight with Gravit’s formidable suite of innovative path, shape and typography tools. Employ helpful styling attributes, or choose from an impressive array of raster and vector effects - the wide range of tools at your disposal means that you can create more complex designs with greater ease. Now the only limit is your imagination!
Our soon-to-be-released cloud app Gravit365 (due late 2014) offers new possibilities for team-wide collaboration at a project level. Joint project management, interactive screen-sharing, peer feedback, support for asset-sharing across multiple documents – a powerful suite of tools that you can use to revolutionise the way you work with your peers – or open up new ways of collaborating with clients!
